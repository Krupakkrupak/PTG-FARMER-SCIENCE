About Us

Cultivating Innovation, Harvesting Success

Founded in 2007, Shree Agro Group has grown into a premier agricultural input company, specializing in fertilizers, pesticides, and seeds. With strong roots in Maharashtra, we have expanded our influence across India and internationally, reaching Oman, Dubai, Kenya, and Russia.

Under the dynamic leadership of our Chairman and Managing Director, Dr. Sagar Ghorpade, Shree Agro Group has revolutionized the agriculture industry by blending innovation with sustainability. Supported by four dedicated sister companies, we are committed to transforming farming practices through high-quality, eco-friendly, and farmer-centric solutions.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide such products to the farmers, using which they are able to get a profitable growth not only in their income but also in their yields without damaging the environment. We are committed to give our customers a superior quality, innovative, advanced and environment friendly range products.

We strive to empower farmers with cutting-edge agricultural solutions that enhance productivity while preserving ecological balance. Our research-driven approach ensures that every product meets the highest standards of efficacy and environmental safety.

Through continuous innovation and sustainable practices, we aim to transform agriculture into a more profitable and environmentally conscious industry. Our commitment extends beyond products to building lasting relationships with farming communities worldwide.
