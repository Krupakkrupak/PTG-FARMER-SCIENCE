About Us

Cultivating Innovation, Harvesting Success

Founded in 2007, Shree Agro Group has grown into a premier agricultural input company, specializing in fertilizers, pesticides, and seeds. With strong roots in Maharashtra, we have expanded our influence across India and internationally, reaching Oman, Dubai, Kenya, and Russia.

Under the dynamic leadership of our Chairman and Managing Director, Dr. Sagar Ghorpade, Shree Agro Group has revolutionized the agriculture industry by blending innovation with sustainability. Supported by four dedicated sister companies, we are committed to transforming farming practices through high-quality, eco-friendly, and farmer-centric solutions.

Our comprehensive product portfolio includes advanced fertilizers that enhance soil fertility, targeted pesticides that protect crops effectively, and premium seeds that ensure higher yields. We continuously invest in research and development to bring cutting-edge agricultural solutions to farmers worldwide.
